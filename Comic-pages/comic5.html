WINDBREAKER

Windbreaker is a manhwa created by Yoo Jae-kyung. It follows Jay, the student president of Sunny High School, who is not only an intelligent student but also an exceptionally skilled cyclist. The story revolves around Jay's cycling adventures, where he encounters friends, romance, and various challenges. Along the way, he meets other cyclists, including some with extraordinary abilities, and faces thrilling competitions and personal growth.

SELLY

Selly is a character in the manhwa Windbreaker and is Jay's girlfriend. They first meet when Jay, while practicing cycling, accidentally collides with Selly. Although their initial encounter is marked by this small accident, their relationship develops as they spend more time together. Selly, with her cheerful personality and supportive nature, becomes an important part of Jay's life, backing him in his cycling career and adventures. Their relationship adds an emotional dimension to Jay's journey throughout the series.
