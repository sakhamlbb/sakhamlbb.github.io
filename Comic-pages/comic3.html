ZHUO FAN

Zhuo Fan is the reincarnation of Zhuo Yifan, the Demon Emperor, who was once a powerful sorcerer betrayed and killed by his disciple. In his new life, he is reborn into a servant boy named Zhuo Fan, carrying the burden of merging his past memories with the identity of the child. Bound by demonic magic, Zhuo Fan must navigate his new life while maintaining his loyalty to the family and young lady he serves. As he struggles to reconcile his former power with his current circumstances, Zhuo Fan embarks on a journey to rebuild his family’s fortunes and reclaim his status as one of the strongest figures on the continent.

CHU QINGCHENG

Chu Qingcheng is a prominent heroine in the story and plays a crucial role in Zhuo Fan's life. She is the daughter of a powerful family and possesses remarkable talent and beauty. Zhuo Fan and Chu Qingcheng first meet during a critical moment when Zhuo Fan, now serving as a humble servant, is involved in protecting the family she belongs to. Despite his lowly status, Zhuo Fan's extraordinary abilities and unwavering loyalty impress Chu Qingcheng. As they grow closer through their shared experiences and challenges, their bond deepens, and Chu Qingcheng becomes a key ally and confidant in Zhuo Fan's journey to reclaim his former power and rebuild his family.
