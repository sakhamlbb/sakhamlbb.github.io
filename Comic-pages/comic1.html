THE BEGINNING AFTER THE END

King Grey has unrivaled strength, wealth, and prestige in a world governed by martial ability. However, solitude lingers closely behind those with great power. Beneath the glamorous exterior of a powerful king lurks the shell of man, devoid of purpose and will. Reincarnated into a new world filled with magic and monsters, the king has a second chance to relive his life. Correcting the mistakes of his past will not be his only challenge, however. Underneath the peace and prosperity of the new world is an undercurrent threatening to destroy everything he has worked for, questioning his role and reason for being born again.

ARTHUR LEYWIN

Arthur Leywin, originally known as King Grey, is the main protagonist of the manhwa "The Beginning After The End." In his previous life, King Grey was a powerful monarch in a highly advanced world. However, despite his immense power and influence, he felt a profound sense of loneliness and dissatisfaction. After his untimely death, he is reincarnated into a new world filled with magic, mythical creatures, and new opportunities. In his new life, Arthur strives to make the most of his second chance, determined to lead a life of purpose and fulfillment. As he grows and develops his magical abilities, he also learns valuable lessons about friendship, love, and the complexities of the world around him.

TESSIA ERALITH

Tessia Eralith is a significant character in "The Beginning After The End" and is known as Arthur Leywin's childhood friend and later becomes his love interest. Tessia is a talented elf princess from the Kingdom of Elenoir, which is renowned for its skilled magic users and rich cultural heritage. She is strong-willed, compassionate, and deeply loyal to her friends and family.Arthur and Tessia first meet when Arthur saves her from a group of kidnappers in the forest. This encounter marks the beginning of their close friendship. Over time, Tessia becomes an important figure in Arthur's life, not just as a friend but also as a source of emotional support and love.
