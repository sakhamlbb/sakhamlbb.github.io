SOLO LEVELING

The manhwa "Solo Leveling," created by the artist Chugong (추공), tells the story of a decade ago when a “Gate” connecting the real world to the world of monsters opened. Ordinary people began gaining the power to hunt monsters inside the Gate, becoming known as “Hunters.” However, not all Hunters are strong. My name is Sung Jin-Woo, a Rank-E Hunter. I am someone who risks my life in the lowest-level dungeons, known as the “Weakest in the World.” Lacking any notable skills, I barely earn enough money by fighting in low-level dungeons… at least until I discover a hidden dungeon with the most difficult challenges in a Rank-D dungeon! In the end, as I faced death, I suddenly gained a strange power: a quest log visible only to me, and the secret to leveling up known only to me! If I train according to my quests and hunt monsters, my level will rise. From being the weakest Hunter, I will become the strongest S-rank Hunter!

SUNG JIN WOO

Sung Jin-Woo is the main protagonist of "Solo Leveling." Initially, he is introduced as a Rank-E Hunter, the lowest and weakest rank in the world of hunters. Despite his status, Jin-Woo is known for his dedication and perseverance, often putting his life on the line in dangerous dungeons just to make ends meet. His skills are minimal, and he struggles with low-paying dungeon quests, making him appear as the weakest hunter among his peers.However, Jin-Woo's life changes dramatically when he encounters a hidden dungeon with the most difficult challenges. On the brink of death, he receives a unique power that grants him a quest log visible only to him and a secret method to level up. With this newfound ability, Jin-Woo starts to rapidly increase his strength and skills, transforming from the weakest hunter into the strongest S-rank Hunter. His journey is one of incredible growth and redemption as he rises to become a legendary figure in the world of hunters.

CHA HAE IN

Cha Hae-In is a key heroine in "Solo Leveling" and plays a significant role in Sung Jin-Woo's life. She is a highly skilled S-rank Hunter known for her exceptional combat abilities and strong sense of duty. As one of the most powerful hunters in the world, she often undertakes high-risk missions and is respected by her peers for her prowess and dedication.Cha Hae-In and Sung Jin-Woo first meet during a high-level dungeon raid where Jin-Woo, by this time, has become a formidable S-rank Hunter. Initially, Hae-In is wary of Jin-Woo due to his mysterious and rapid rise in strength, but she quickly comes to admire his strength and determination. Their relationship develops as they work together on various missions, and Hae-In becomes a close ally and confidant for Jin-Woo. Their bond grows deeper as they face challenges side by side, and Hae-In’s respect for Jin-Woo evolves into a more profound connection.
